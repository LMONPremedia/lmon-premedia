🦉 OTTO TIP

Dot gain is influenced by the printing process, substrate, ink, pressure and screen ruling.

Always calibrate your press and compensate for expected dot gain during prepress.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • Newspapers exhibit higher dot gain than coated stocks.
  • Flexographic printing generally has more dot gain than offset.
  • ICC profiles compensate for expected dot gain.
  • Excessive dot gain reduces shadow detail.
