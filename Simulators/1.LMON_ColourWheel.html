💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • Complementary — Creates maximum contrast.
  • Analogous — Creates natural harmony.
  • Triadic — Offers balanced vibrancy.
  • Tetradic — Rich and complex, but harder to balance.
  • Monochromatic — Simple, elegant and always works.
