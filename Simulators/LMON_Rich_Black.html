Rich Black

C60 M40 Y40 K100
Recommended for:
Backgrounds • Posters • Packaging

🦉 OTTO TIP

Use 100K Black for small text and fine details to avoid registration issues. Use Rich Black for large solid areas where a deeper, darker black is required.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • 100K Black prints using only the black plate.
  • Rich Black combines CMYK inks for greater density.
  • Rich Black should not be used for small text.
  • Different printers may recommend different Rich Black recipes.
