🦉 OTTO TIP

Overprint allows inks to print on top of one another, while knockout removes the underlying colour before printing. Choosing the correct setting avoids unexpected colour changes.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • Black text commonly overprints in commercial printing.
  • Overprinting Cyan and Yellow produces Green.
  • Incorrect overprint settings can disappear in print.
  • Always preview overprints before exporting a PDF.
