🦉 OTTO TIP

Small changes in screen angle can produce visible moiré patterns. Proper screen angle selection is essential for high-quality halftone printing.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • CMYK screens use different angles to minimise moiré.
  • Photographs are more sensitive to moiré than flat graphics.
  • Digital screening algorithms reduce moiré risk.
  • Incorrect screen angles can make prints appear textured.
