🦉 OTTO TIP

In commercial printing, tolerances are often between 0.05 mm and 0.25 mm depending on process and substrate.

💡 DID YOU KNOW?

  • A 0.1 mm registration error can be visible on small text.
  • Black is often used as the reference plate.
  • Paper stretch can affect registration.
  • Modern presses use automatic register control.
